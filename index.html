QUESTIONS

Common questions from the shop floor.

How long do these actually last outdoors?
Our 3M 2080 and Oracal 751 films are rated 5–7 years with proper installation. We’ve seen them go 8+ on daily drivers in harsh climates. We laminate everything we can for extra protection.
Do you do full custom designs?
Yes. Send us your sketch, logo file (AI, EPS, PDF, or even a clean phone photo), or just describe what you want. We vector it properly and send a proof for approval before cutting.
Can I put these on my motorcycle or helmet?
Absolutely. We have specific high-performance reflective and solvent-resistant films made for that. Just select the “Helmets & Gear” category or tell us in the custom form.
What file types work best for custom work?
Vector files (AI, EPS, SVG) are ideal. High-resolution PNGs and clean PDFs also work. We will redraw anything that needs to be vectorized at no extra charge on orders over $65.
Do you offer bulk or wholesale pricing?
Yes. Fleets, shops, and clubs get better pricing. Email us directly or use the custom order form and mention quantity. We take care of our people.
